# Waabanong Iteration Features
**Source:** Approved client deliverables — update this file when iteration scope changes.

These are the Features lists that appear in every weekly update and status report.
The content is static week-to-week; update only when scope is formally confirmed to have changed.

---

## I1 - System Infrastructure & Environments

**Features**

- Set up Development, Testing, and Production environments

---

## I2 - Login & Authorization

**Features**

- Microsoft 365–based authentication
- User login

---

## I3 - Client, Member & Family Data

**Features**

- Centralized person records (children, adults, limited profiles)
  - Duplicate prevention
- Family and household structures
  - Multi-generation relationships
- Reciprocal relationship logic
- Global search across people and families

---

## I4 - Intake Management & Automated Notifications

**Features**

- Structured intake workflows with mandatory fields
- Intake status tracking and lifecycle
- Supervisor review and approval processes
- Ability to resolve intake without opening a case
- Escalation from intake to case creation
- Printable intake records
- Automated notifications:
  - In-application alerts
  - Defined external channels (email/messaging)

---

## I5 A - Case File Management & Signs of Safety

**Features**

- Case file creation and lifecycle management
- Assignment of caseworkers and supervisors
- Case reassignment with approval
- Signs of Safety–aligned workflows, including:
  - Placement
  - Structured assessments
  - Safety goals and planning
  - Ongoing review checkpoints
- Visibility into historical and related cases
- Case notes for visits, services, and interactions
- Communication tracking across intake and case contexts
  - Document management: Person, Intake & Case

---

## I5 B – Abuse Investigations

**Features**

- Triggers and integration with Intake module
- Investigation workflow
- Investigation document management

---

## I6 - Data Cleaning and Migration

**Features**

- Develop a data migration strategy from FAMCare into Waabanong
- Support PCFS as they perform data cleanup
- Execute trial data migrations in a sandbox environment
- Perform live data migration

---

## I7 - Financial Tracking & Exports for Sage

**Features (Potential scope may include)**

- Intake-level emergency supports
- Case-level budgets
- Spending caps and approvals
- Export files to import into Sage 300

---

## I8 - My Dashboard & Reporting

**Features (Potential scope may include)**

- My Dashboard (simple, quick reference to key items)
- Tasks & alerts
- Reporting (if required and not covered in other iterations)
